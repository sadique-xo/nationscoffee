## Visual Direction

### Interior Aesthetic (from photos)
- Corrugated tin ceiling, white walls, warm wood frames
- Minimal, line-art illustrations on menu boards (barista, coffee beans, pour-over, iced drink, person with giant coffee cup)
- Leaf/botanical line illustrations along bottom of menu boards
- Warm track lighting, cozy intimate space (~25 seats)
- Black matte plates for food presentation
- Natural wood tables
- Overall: **Minimal · Warm · Artisanal · Faith-inspired**

### Website Design Direction
- **NOT** loud/trendy café chain aesthetic
- **YES** clean, warm, minimal - like the menu boards themselves
- White/cream backgrounds with olive green accents
- Line-art illustrations as decorative elements (match menu board style)
- Photography-forward (food shots on black plates look great)
- Faith element subtle but present (the blessing quote, "Faith. Hope. Love. Peace.")
- Mobile-first - most customers will access via Instagram link

### Illustration Style (from menu boards)
The menu boards feature a consistent hand-drawn line art style:
- Pour-over coffee dripper (Hario V60)
- Iced drink glass with straw
- Person pushing/hugging a giant coffee cup
- Coffee bean motifs
- Botanical leaf border at bottom
- All in black line art on cream/white background

## Photography Notes (from Google Photos)

Visible in customer photos:
- Iced coffees (dark cold brew + orange juice layered drink) on black plates
- Frappes with striped paper straws
- Cake slices (carrot cake, butter pound cake) on black plates
- Mojitos/mocktails in clear glasses
- Cheesecake slices beautifully plated
- Cozy wooden table settings

**Key food styling:** Black matte plates are the signature - everything is plated on them. Use this in website photography direction.
